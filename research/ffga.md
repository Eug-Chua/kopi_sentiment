# FFGA Framework

### FEARS
- What keeps them up at night
- Risks they're afraid to take
- Negative outcomes they want to avoid
- Anxieties and uncertainties

**Example insights:**
- "Afraid of wasting money on the wrong tool"
- "Worried about losing data if they switch platforms"
- "Anxious about falling behind competitors"

### FRUSTRATIONS
- Daily pain points and irritations
- Inefficient processes
- Current solution limitations
- Time/money wasters

**Example insights:**
- "Current CRM is too complex, takes 30 min to add a contact"
- "Tools don't integrate, manually copying data"
- "Customer support is non-existent"

### GOALS
- Immediate objectives
- Problems they're trying to solve
- Metrics they want to improve
- Short-term targets

**Example insights:**
- "Need to close 10 deals this quarter"
- "Want to reduce time spent on admin from 10hrs to 2hrs/week"
- "Trying to automate email follow-ups"

### ASPIRATIONS
- Ideal future state
- Ultimate success vision
- Transformational outcomes
- Deeper motivations

**Example insights:**
- "Want to build a business that runs without them"
- "Dream of having more time with family"
- "Aspire to be seen as an industry leader"
