# FFO Framework

### FEARS
- What keeps them up at night
- Risks they're afraid to take
- Negative outcomes they want to avoid
- Anxieties and uncertainties

**Example insights:**
- "Afraid of wasting money on the wrong tool"
- "Worried about losing data if they switch platforms"
- "Anxious about falling behind competitors"

### FRUSTRATIONS
- Daily pain points and irritations
- Inefficient processes
- Current solution limitations
- Time/money wasters

**Example insights:**
- "Current CRM is too complex, takes 30 min to add a contact"
- "Tools don't integrate, manually copying data"
- "Customer support is non-existent"

### OPTIMISM
- Hopes for improvement
- Positive outlook on current trajectory
- Confidence in future outcomes
- Silver linings they see

**Example insights:**
- "Hope prices stabilize soon"
- "New policies seem promising for young families"
- "Singapore's economy is resilient, we'll adapt"
